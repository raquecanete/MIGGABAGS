Our Genesis

Client Experiences

MG EXCLUSIVE emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create luxury that matters. Founded in 2015 by Marco Gianni, we set out to challenge industry norms by combining exquisite craftsmanship with sustainable practices.

We recognized that true luxury shouldn't come at the expense of our planet or people. Frustrated by the industry's wastefulness, we pioneered a closed-loop production system that has since become our signature.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Miggabags into what it is today

2018 – The Spark

Started in Cebu by passionate Hospitality Management students with a dream of sustainable local products.

2019 – First Product Launch

Released our first handcrafted eco-bags made from local materials at a community fair.

2021 – Campus to Community

Expanded to nearby schools and local markets, promoting reusable bags and youth entrepreneurship.

2022 – Collaboration with Local Artisans

Partnered with weavers and craftsmen in Visayas to create limited-edition designs.

2024 – Online Launch & Eco Recognition

Launched our e-commerce site and received a local sustainability award for green innovation.

Giving Back

At Miggabags Enterprises, we believe that every purchase should uplift communities and help protect the planet we all share.

Tree Planting Initiatives

We plant trees across Cebu in partnership with youth groups and LGUs to restore our local environment.

Empowering Local Artisans

We collaborate with Visayan weavers and sewers, ensuring fair wages and preserving cultural craftsmanship.

Eco-Bag Return Program

Customers can return used Miggabags for recycling, repair, or donation to communities in need.
