Quick answers about Miggabags Enterprises

Miggabags products are handcrafted by skilled artisans in the Philippines, India, and Indonesia. We collaborate with small, community-based workshops to ensure every piece is made with care, quality, and ethical labor practices.

We are committed to sustainability through the use of natural, upcycled, and biodegradable materials. Our designs are timeless, reducing waste from fast fashion trends. We also partner with eco-projects to offset our carbon footprint.

Yes, Miggabags offers free repairs within 12 months of purchase and discounted repair services after that. We accept returns within 30 days, no questions asked. We also offer a return-to-recycle program to reduce waste.
